### üéØ Goal of the Dashboard

This project analyzes *Hospital Emergency Room (ER)* data for *9,200+ patients* using *Power BI* to drive operational excellence. The primary objective is to track key KPIs like *Avg Wait Time* and *Patient Satisfaction, identify **peak operational demand* (Mondays, 7 PM/11 PM), and inform strategic decisions for *resource allocation and efficiency*.

## üåü Features / Highlights

* *KPI Tracking:* Monitoring of core metrics like *Avg Wait Time (35.3 Min)* and *Patient Satisfaction Score (4.99)*.
* *Peak Demand Analysis:* Identification of the busiest days and hours (*Mondays, 7 PM, and 11 PM*) crucial for staffing optimization.
* *Demographic Segmentation:* Detailed views of patient load segmented by Age, Gender, and Race.
* *Referral Flow:* Tracking patient disposition (Admitted vs. Not Admitted) and analysis of referrals to departments like *General Practice* and *Orthopaedics*.

---

## üö® Problems Addressed

The dashboard provides solutions for critical operational challenges:

* *Long Wait Times:* Pinpointing process bottlenecks contributing to high average wait times, which directly impacts patient experience.
* *Inefficient Staffing:* Providing clear data on peak hours to prevent understaffing during busy times and reduce unnecessary costs during slow periods.
* *Performance Monitoring:* Establishing a clear benchmark for measuring and comparing monthly performance against targets.

---

## üö∂ Walkthrough of Key Visuals (Views)

The dashboard is structured into four interactive views:

1.  *Consolidated View:* High-level summary of all 19 months (Apr 2023 - Oct 2024), showing overall KPIs and broad demographic trends.
2.  *Monthly View:* Granular, month-specific operational performance analysis.
3.  *Patient Details:* A row-level, searchable table for specific patient record lookup and validation.
4.  *Key Takeaways:* A descriptive analysis summarizing the most important findings and business implications.

---

## üí° Impact and Insights

* *Resource Optimization:* Clear identification of peak periods (Mon/7 PM/11 PM) allows managers to schedule staff effectively, improving service efficiency without increasing overall budget.
* *Service Improvement:* Provides empirical evidence to target specific process changes, ultimately aiming to reduce the Avg Wait Time and maintain the high Patient Satisfaction Score.
* *Strategic Planning:* Referral analysis highlights the specialties in highest demand from the ER, aiding long-term hospital resource and capacity planning.
